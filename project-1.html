Project: Dress Up

For this class I decided to make a magnetic dress-up kit for kids. The clothes we wear and the way we dress is an important part of our everyday experience. Young kids in particular often develop their individuality through their clothes, and use getting dressed as a way to express their culture, personality, and individuality. My Dress-up kit provides young kids with a source of entertainment, that easily allows them to experiment with clothes. In addition, it provides a break from technology, and encourages hands on creativity.
