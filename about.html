    Who is she?

    Yohahnah, pronounced Yo-haw-nuh is a 4th year student at IDEA School of Design. Her focus is branding, with a particular interest in design for social good campaigns. In Yohahnah's books, nothing beats design that deeply understands and affirms its audience. When she can't be found crafting something, you can catch Yohahnah out taking an evening stroll, reading interactive fiction, trying out new plant-based recipes, or fussing over her plants.
